peter parkr is an app brought to you by Xpanda Portal L.L.C, a mainland dubai-based company founded and managed by two childhood friends (Omar Abou Chama & Shuaib AI Sulaimani). There goal was simple: use technology for the betterment of world and for that to happen they needed to solve a problem that was making people miserable, a problem that no one has attempted to yet solve, at least not in a way that really mattered.


we developed peter parkr because like most people, looking for parking was taking far too much valuable time out of our lives and we decided to put a meaningful end to it. nobody needs to waste a substantial amount of time looking for a spot to park their car in, or abondon their car for fear of not finding parking, or park illegally and then pay for it dearly. we decided that this serious problem needs a serious approach so why not apply technology and change it in a way that benefits all. this is why we are proud to present to you this mobile app that can solve your parking problem and take away all the stresses that come with it.
